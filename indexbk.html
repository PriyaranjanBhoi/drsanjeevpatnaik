Sanjeev Patnaik, MS.

Dr. Sanjeev Patnaik is one of the leading Orthopedic & Joint Replacement surgeons of India, having undergone extensive training and fellowships in India and abroad. His pioneering work on Minimally Invasive Hip and Knee replacement surgery have won him numerous accolades across the globe.
With over 18 years of experience in Hip & Knee Replacement Surgery (both Primary & Revision Surgeries) he is an active contributing Faculty at numerous National & International Conferences, along with performed live surgeries at Orthopaedic Clinical & scientific meetings.
